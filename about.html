Education

  • Doctoral degree in Mathematics. Bielefeld University (Germany), 2006–2009.
  • Engineering degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Systems. Escuela Superior Politécnica del Litoral (Ecuador), 1999–2005.

Academic Experience

  • Professor of Applied Mathematics, Faculty of Natural Sciences and Mathematics, Escuela Superior Politécnica del Litoral (Ecuador) since March 2017. Teaching responsibilities: Calculus, Linear Algebra, Numerical Analysis, Dynamical Systems.
  • Invited Professor at the Faculty of Mathematics and Sciences, University of Koblenz-Landau (Germany) from October 2019 to March 2020 (Link). Teaching and research activities under the Master's Program "Mathematical Modeling of Complex Systems".
  • Director and founder of the Center for Applied Dynamical Systems and Computational Methods (CADSCOM), Escuela Superior Politécnica del Litoral (Ecuador) since March 2017 (Link).
  • Research Fellow at the Center for Dynamics (CFD), Dresden University of Technology (Germany) from May 2014 to February 2017 (Link). Collaboration with the Center for Advancing Electronics Dresden (CFAED) in the area of mathematical modelling of Chemical Information Processing Systems.
  • Research Fellow at the Centre for Applied Dynamics Research (CADR), University of Aberdeen (United Kingdom) from May 2012 to April 2014 (Link). Contribution to the CADR research areas of Resonance Enhanced Drilling, Rotor Dynamics, Impacting Systems and Drill-String Vibrations. Organizer and chair of the CADR seminar series.
  • Director of the postgraduate program in Mathematics, Escuela Superior Politécnica del Litoral (Ecuador) from July 2009 to April 2012.
  • Researcher and lecturer at the Institute of Mathematical Sciences, Escuela Superior Politécnica del Litoral (Ecuador) from July 2009 to April 2012.
  • Guest member of the Research Group for Numerical Analysis of Dynamical Systems, Bielefeld University (Germany) since 2009 (Link).
